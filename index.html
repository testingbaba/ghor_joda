About Us

Alma Mater Matters Pvt Ltd (Ghar Joda) – Exclusive Lounge to Explore Matrimonial Alliance Among Alumni of Premier Educational Institutions. The website was launched in April 2014. We now have members in India and 50 other countries. One of the biggest differentiators of our website, other than its niche segment, is that we authenticate each profile on the basis of education by screening their institution’s degree, ID-card/mark sheet etc.
